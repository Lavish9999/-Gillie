Gillie Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Gillie is a local-first quit-vaping wellness companion. It does not require an account to use the current app.

Data Storage

Your quit timer, check-ins, cravings, goals, Reef items, and app preferences are stored on your device using local app storage. Gillie does not currently run a user account system or upload your quit data to a Gillie server.

Purchases

Gillie Plus purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. Apple may process purchase information according to Apple's privacy practices. Gillie only needs to know whether an active entitlement exists so premium features can be unlocked.

Future Sync Features

If Gillie adds optional cloud sync, accounts, analytics, or backup features in the future, this policy will be updated before those features are used.

Wellness Notice

Gillie is a wellness and behavior-change companion. It is not medical treatment, does not diagnose or treat any condition, and does not replace advice from a doctor, therapist, quitline, or other qualified professional.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support instructions on the Gillie Support page.
