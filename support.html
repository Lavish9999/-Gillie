Gillie Support

Gillie is a supportive nicotine-quitting wellness companion centered around a small aquatic buddy and practical daily tools.

Contact Gillie Support

Before contacting support

Open You → Export diagnostics and attach or paste the exported report when reporting a bug. The report excludes journal notes, pet names, and free-text slip details.

Protecting your progress

Open You → Export Gillie backup to save a private JSON backup containing your recovery history, check-ins, cravings, Reef items, and preferences. Use Import Gillie backup to restore it later. Gillie Plus is never copied from a backup; Apple re-verifies the subscription.

FAQ

  • Does Gillie require an account? No. The current app is local-first and does not require sign-in.
  • Where is my quit data stored? On your device and in any Gillie backup file you deliberately export.
  • How do reminders work? Gillie uses local iPhone notifications. Enable them in Gillie and allow notification permission when asked.
  • How do I restore Gillie Plus? Open Gillie Plus and select Restore purchases while signed into the Apple ID used to subscribe.
  • How do I cancel? Open You → Manage subscription, or manage subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.
  • Is Gillie medical treatment? No. Gillie is a general wellness companion and does not replace medical advice or professional cessation support.
