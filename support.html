Gillie Support

Gillie is a supportive quit-vaping wellness companion centered around a small aquatic buddy and daily habit tools.

Contact

For app support, App Store questions, or bug reports, contact the developer through the email address listed on Gillie's App Store product page.

FAQ

  • Does Gillie require an account? No. The current app is local-first and does not require sign-in.
  • Where is my quit data stored? Your current quit data is stored on your device unless future optional sync features are added.
  • Is Gillie medical treatment? No. Gillie is a wellness companion and does not diagnose, treat, or replace medical advice.
  • How do I restore Gillie Plus? Use the restore purchase option in the Gillie Plus screen in the iOS app.
  • What if I need more help quitting? Consider contacting a doctor, therapist, or local quitline for professional support.
