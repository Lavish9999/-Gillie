Gillie Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Gillie is a local-first quit-vaping wellness companion. The current app does not require an account and does not sell personal information.

Quit and wellness information

Your quit timer, check-ins, craving records, slip reflections, goals, Reef items, reminders, and preferences are stored on your device. Gillie does not currently upload this information to a Gillie server.

Private diagnostics

Gillie keeps a limited on-device diagnostic log to help identify crashes and whether important app flows work. The log excludes journal notes, pet names, free-text slip details, contacts, advertising identifiers, precise location, and browsing activity. Diagnostics remain on your device unless you deliberately use “Export diagnostics” and choose to share them.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Gillie schedules reminders locally on your device. The current app does not need a remote push-notification server to deliver these reminders. You can turn reminders off in Gillie or in iPhone Settings.

Purchases

Gillie Plus purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. Apple processes purchase and billing information under Apple’s privacy practices. Gillie reads verified StoreKit entitlement status so paid features can be unlocked.

No advertising tracking

Gillie does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

Wellness notice

Gillie supports general wellness and behavior change. It is not medical treatment, does not diagnose or treat a condition, and does not replace a doctor, therapist, pharmacist, quitline, or emergency service.

Your choices

You may erase Gillie data from the app’s settings. Because current quit data is local-first, deleting the app or erasing its storage can permanently remove that history.
