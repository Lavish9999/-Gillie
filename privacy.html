Gillie Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Gillie is a local-first nicotine-quitting wellness companion. The current app does not require an account and does not sell personal information.

Quit and wellness information

Your quit timer, check-ins, craving records, slip reflections, goals, Reef progression and items, reminders, and preferences are stored on your device. Gillie does not currently upload this information to a Gillie server.

Backups you control

Gillie can create a portable JSON backup when you deliberately choose Export Gillie backup. The file may contain your recovery history, check-ins, cravings, Reef progression and items, and preferences. You control where that file is saved or shared. Importing a backup replaces the current on-device Gillie history. Subscription entitlement is excluded and is re-verified with Apple.

Private diagnostics

Gillie keeps a limited on-device diagnostic log to help identify crashes and whether important app flows work. The log may include app version, device model, operating-system version, event names, timestamps, and technical performance reports. It excludes journal notes, pet names, free-text slip details, contacts, advertising identifiers, precise location, and browsing activity. Diagnostics remain on your device unless you deliberately use Export diagnostics and choose to share them.

Retention and deletion

On-device Gillie information remains until you erase it, delete the app, or replace it by importing a backup. Choosing Erase Everything removes Gillie recovery data, preferences, and locally stored diagnostics from the device. Any backup or diagnostic file you previously saved or shared is outside Gillie’s control and must be deleted from its saved location separately. Gillie does not provide an account or server-side profile that requires a separate deletion request.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Gillie schedules reminders locally on your device. The current app does not need a remote push-notification server to deliver these reminders. You can turn reminders off in Gillie or in iPhone Settings.

Purchases

Gillie Plus purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. Apple processes purchase and billing information under Apple’s privacy practices. Gillie reads verified StoreKit entitlement status so paid features can be unlocked.

No advertising tracking

Gillie does not use third-party advertising SDKs, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not sell personal information.

Wellness notice

Gillie supports general wellness and behavior change. It is not medical treatment, does not diagnose or treat a condition, and does not replace a doctor, therapist, pharmacist, quitline, or emergency service.

Your choices

You can turn reminders off, export or erase local information, and choose whether to share a backup or diagnostic report. Because Gillie is local-first, deleting the app or erasing its storage can permanently remove history that was not preserved in a backup.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Gillie Support or visit the Gillie Support page.
